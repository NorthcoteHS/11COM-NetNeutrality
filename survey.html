Net Neutrality Survey

What is Net Neutrality by definition is the principle that Internet service providers should enable access to all content and applications regardless of the source, and without favouring or blocking particular products or websites. Now you may be thinking what this means in a laymans terms is that ISPs or interenet service providers can not restrict what you do on the internet internet service providers are companies like optus, telstra, dodo, iinet, belong and many more. More importantly you have probably heard of net neutrality before on the news being the repeal of net neutrality in the U.S. in november of 2017 it was announced that the US government was going through a process of repealing net neutrality and evetnutally they were succesful giving internet service providers much more control over the US's internet connection.
