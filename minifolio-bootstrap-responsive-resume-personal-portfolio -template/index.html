what is Net Neutrality?

the principle that Internet service providers should enable access to all content and applications regardless of the source, and without favouring or blocking particular products or websites. This is the definition of net neutrality in other words it means that ISPs (internet service providers) can not restrict what websites you use and also cannot change speeds of certain websites for instance if you were on telstra internet without net neutrality you could possibly not have access to certain websites or they could be slowed down.

You may have heard of the repeal of net neutrality in america around november to december 2017 and how much uproar their was. You may think that a repeal of such a key feature sounds ridiculous and you probably can't understand why people would want the removal of such a important feature but you might be surprised to know their are disadvantages to net neutrality because with it their is no filter on the internet and hate speech and porn can roam free all over the internet for young children and others can easily access which will likely negatively influence them.
