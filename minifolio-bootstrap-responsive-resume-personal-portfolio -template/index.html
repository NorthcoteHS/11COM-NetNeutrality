Net Neutrality


Scroll to see my work.

The disadvantages of repealing net neutrality far outway the advantages as it gives the internet service providers too much control over the internet when the internet should be a free space. This affects everyone from ordinary people to advertisers and businesses negatively. For example ordinary people may no longer be able to access their favourite websites (pornhub,reddit,4chan) as they may be seen as controversial or bad for business, without net neutrality ISP (internet service providers) can monetise access to certain websites. The repeal affects small businesses and advertisers too as their influence on the internet can also be censored by being slowed down for example if big jim has a company called beauty and care it might not be as accessible without a certain internet package.
