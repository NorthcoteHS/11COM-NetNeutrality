Solutions

There are three possible solutions to net neutrality, each of which have their own advantages and disadvantages. You can either have or not have net neutrality or you can regulate it. Having net neutrality would mean that some companies would get faster speeds, but others would get slower speeds. Not having net neutrality would make it so everyone can have equal access to the internet, but you wouldn’t be able to block websites from anyone. You could also regulate it so that inappropriate websites can be blocked from kids, but this would mean paying more for internet access.
