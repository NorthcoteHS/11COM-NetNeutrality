Advantages of Net Neutrality

With net neutrality the internet is a free space where you can say everything you want with little to no consequence and intervention from your service provider or the government. With net neutrality the internet can be used for anything you want and you can access any sites you want in any country at any time. Also with net neutrality their are platforms where you can speak your opinion it is very likely that if net neutrality was repealed that this ability would be revoked. With net neutrality large companies do not have an advantage in connection speed to small businesses so its a level playing field though, if net neutrality is removed than it is very likely that small businesses will no longer get the attention that large businesses get and the attention that they deserve.

Net neutrality is a key feature of the internet and its an essential to the freedom of the internet if you remove net neutrality the internet will lose its most valuable feature. This will end many things in the internet as the main draw for eemany to the internet is the freedom and the fact they can do anything they want access anything they want, and not pay any extra for internet speeds or access to websites. The internet may have been made for the government but it is no longer a tool, and should not be controlled by anyone especially by the people who want us to pay them for internet (ISP).
