Disadvantages of Net Neutrality

Many people believe censorship is necessary on the internet as they believe that content like porn and offensive material should be censored and thus closed off with slower internet speeds so children cannot access these sights. And so offensive opinions aren't public knowledge which is a good thing per say but you also have to realise if you censor some opinions thats removing free speech. It could also make internet cheaper to some people who only want specific things from the internet for instance if someone only uses the internet for news or shopping this could in theory be cheaper.
